About Us

Here to quench all your cravings, we at Barish Coffee promise to give you an experience you will keep coming back for. We not only provide absolute lip-smacking treats but also an experience for all your senses. One can come with only an empty stomach but be assured to leave with a wholesome fulfillment of all they can perceive, as we pay attention to the ambiance, taste, hygiene, and experience, ensuring we don’t miss out on any aspect of having a customer leaving anything less than fully satisfied.

We are famously known for our Broasted Chicken, Al-Faham, and our in-house coffee made with love from scratch. We have been loved by the public for six years now and plan on spreading this love outside our four existing branches very soon. Roasted and grilled to absolute perfection, our chicken is best-selling for its tones of fiery spice, tasteful seasoning, and hot serving. Along with this, we brew our own coffee with love and care for an art that we’ve mastered over the years. With the perfect combination of sweet and strong, our coffee can cure whatever blues you’re facing that day, bringing warm joy to the world around.
